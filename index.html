Mary Kom

Mangte Chungneijang Mary Kom aka Magnificent Mary

Here is some information about this magnifient lady:

  • Born on 24 November 1982 · Kagathei, Churachandpur, Manipur, India
  • Her journey began as an 18-year-old, where she reached the final in the 48kg category but secured a silver medal.
  • She is the only Indian Boxer to participate in Olympics 2012 and win a bronze medal.
  • Ranked #4 in the AIBA World Women’s Ranking for Flyweight Category.
  • She received the Padma Shri (India’s fourth-highest civilian honor) in 2010 for her significant contributions to sports.
  • Mary Kom’s legacy extends beyond her medals; she has inspired generations and remains an icon in the world of boxing.
